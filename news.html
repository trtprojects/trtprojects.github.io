News & Events

February 5, 2024

Dose Insight at MD&M West

Dose Insight will be attending MD&M West in Anaheim from Feb. 6-8, 2024! We will be at SteriTek's booth (2566). Find out how virtual dose mapping can save you time and money!

November 21, 2023

Dose Insight receives $1.4M in non-dilutive funding

We are happy to announce that we received a $1.4 million Phase II SBIR grant from the FDA! This funding will allow us to fully develop our virtual dose mapping software. This grant was in response to a call from the FDA, wherein they expressed the need to develop computer modelling tools that help with regulatory decision making.

October 31, 2023

2023 Fermilab Medical Device Sterilization Workshop

We were fortunate to give a presentation entitled 'Finding the optimal sterilization configuration using simulations' at the 2023 Medical Device Sterilization Workshop. Please use the link to watch a video of our presentation!

December 12, 2022

2022 Fermilab Medical Device Sterilization Workshop

Dose Insight outlined our concept of Design for Sterilization in a presentation at the 2022 Fermilab Medical Device Sterilization Workshop. In this presentation, we drew parallels with other industries, such as injection molding and data center cooling, that evolved from rules-of-thumb based design to modeling-based design.

December 8, 2022

Talk at the ASTM committee week, New Orleans 2022

We had the honor to present our thoughts on Design for Sterilization to the standards committee E61 (radiation processing). The talk was well received and sparked a very interesting discussion with lots of new inputs. We are sharing the slides here.



December 7, 2022

Plenary talk at IMRP 20

We shared our progress in developing our virtual dose mapping tool at the International Meeting on Radiation Processing (IMRP) in 2022. In this talk, we shared some background into the concept of virtual dose mapping and presented different ways to accelerated radiation-matter simulations. A pre-recorded version of the talk can be watched here.
