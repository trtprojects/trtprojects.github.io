About

Where we came from and where we're going.

Our Team

Dose Insight was founded by an established team of physicists, engineers, and entrepreneurs. Throughout our decades working in medical device development, we have seen first-hand the risks posed by sterilization validation. By leveraging our proven expertise in high performance modeling software, we created Dose Insight to solve an unmet need in the medical device community.

Our Clients

Dose Insight has worked with companies of all shapes and sizes, from startups to Fortune 500s. From cutting-edge prototypes to market-leading products. But whether you are big or small, the horror stories are the same: Delays in production due to failures during sterilization validation. Trying dozens of packing configurations to meet the dose uniformity requirements. Dose map after dose map. Dose Insight's modeling software has enabled our clients to pass the hurdle of steriliation validation with the comfort of knowing what the dose map will look like before actually measuring it.

Our Future

We are working on launching a subscription service where users can harness the power of Dose Insight for themselves. Please get in touch to find out more.
