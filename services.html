Get your virtual dose maps today!

Here's how it works

We typically kick it off with an introductory meeting to review and understands your goal for the project. This helps us be aligned to ensure we are able to bring you the most value we can.

To run the simulations, we need you to provide us with the following:

  1. A CAD model of your device/packaging. We accept CAD files from all major vendors.
  2. The names of all the materials you are using. This could be included in the CAD model, or as a separate BOM. If this is a preliminary design, don't worry if your material choices aren't finalized yet; we can work with you to make some educated guesses.
  3. Information about your e-beam sterilization facility, such as the beam energy. Again, if you haven't chosen a beamline yet, no problem!

We then run our validated simulations in the cloud. This means there are no hardware requirements when working with Dose Insight to perform virtual dose mapping. No investment in an expensive compute cluster necessary!

Finally, we provide you with a dose report and a wrap up meeting to go over your results. The dose report contains the results of the virtual dose mapping simulations, as well as our analysis, including the predicted minimum and maximum dose and Dose Uniformity Ratio (DUR). We spend a lot of time looking at virtual dose maps, so we leverage our expertise to distill the rich information contained in a virtual dose map to the essentials that you need to make a business decision.
